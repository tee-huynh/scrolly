what are conditions?
conditions are requirements that a company must complete to be allowed to construct, operate and abandon a project. a large portion of the NEB's compliance work focuses on overseeing companies to make sure they comply with the conditions the NEB has set.
how many conditions are there?
conditions on a project can vary depending on the situation. some projects have one to five, and some have hundreds. overall, there are 4,123 conditions from 2010-2018.

what are some conditions about?
conditions will vary for every project and express different intentions. these are the top three words that appear the most in conditions.
when is a company required to meet its conditions?
condition timelines are project-specific. the NEB can impose conditions throughout the life cycle of the project. sometimes, companies must meet conditions before they can begin a new project or a new phase of a project.
